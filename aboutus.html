SoloCloud Hosting – Our Journey

December 2023 Founded

I, SoloPlayz, started SoloCloud Hosting with a vision to provide reliable, affordable, trusted, and high performance hosting. At launch, our Minecraft plans were available only in India.

February 2024 First Plans Live

Launched our first Minecraft server plans with competitive prices and reliable uptime for Indian users.

March 2024 Community Support Begins

Started building a community of players and server owners; gathered feedback to improve our services.

April 2024 Hardware Upgrades

Upgraded our servers with better CPUs and SSD storage for faster performance.

November 2024 Germany Node Added

Expanded globally by introducing a Germany location for European users.

December 2024 Improved Support System

Implemented faster and more responsive support to help users troubleshoot issues quickly.

January 2025 One Year Anniversary

Celebrated 1 year of SoloCloud Hosting with continuous improvements and loyal users.

March 2025 VPS Hosting Services

Introduced VPS hosting and billing services alongside Minecraft hosting.

June 2025 Enterprise Grade Servers

Upgraded our infrastructure with high-frequency AMD CPUs and better uptime reliability.

August 2025 Community Milestones

Reached 4,500+ active users globally; refined our plans based on community feedback.

October 2025 Affordable & Performance Plans Live

Upgraded our infrastructure with Intel and Ryzen high-frequency CPUs across multiple locations.

November 2025 Singapore Node Added

Launched Singapore location to serve users in Asia with even faster Minecraft servers.

Present – 2025

After 2 years of growth, SoloCloud is a trusted hosting provider for gamers and creators worldwide, still led by me, SoloPlayz, focusing on high performance, affordable, and reliable hosting.

A Note from the Founder – SoloPlayz

It’s been an amazing 2 years of trusted hosting with SoloCloud. Thanks to all our users for supporting us — we remain committed to reliable, high performance, and affordable hosting.
